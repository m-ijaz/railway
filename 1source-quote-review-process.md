1. Overview
The Quote Review UI is a web-based interface hosted on Railway that gives sales reps full control over every quote before it reaches the customer. After the AI automatically sources prices and builds a draft quote, the rep receives an internal email with a unique review link. They open it in any browser — no login required — review all line items, make any adjustments needed, and click Approve & Send. Only then does the customer receive the quote.

4. Adding Parts — Two Methods
When a sales rep needs to add a part that was not in the customer's original request, they have two options available directly inside the Quote Review UI.



Best for: parts the rep knows from experience, samples, special pricing from a supplier, or parts with a pre-negotiated rate.

Rep clicks '+ Add Part' button at the bottom of the line items table
A new empty row appears with editable fields
Rep enters: MPN, Quantity, Unit Cost
Default margin (25%) is applied automatically — rep can adjust
Sell price calculates instantly
Part is added to the quote and totals update
Rep proceeds to Preview or Approve as normal





Best for: parts the rep wants to add but needs a current market price for first. The system searches OEMsTrade and FindChips in real time and returns the best available price.

Rep clicks '+ Add Part' button
A new empty row appears
Rep enters: MPN and Quantity only
Rep clicks the 'Source Price' button (magnifying glass icon)
System searches OEMsTrade + FindChips in real time (takes 5-15 seconds)
Best price found is populated automatically into the Unit Cost field
Supplier name and source are shown next to the price
Rep can override the price if needed
Default margin applied, sell price calculated
Part added to quote, totals update
Rep proceeds to Preview or Approve

Every action taken during the quote review process is logged automatically in the GHL opportunity notes. This ensures full traceability for ISO 9001 compliance.
